Intro

My name is Alex Moerschbacher and I'm an Information Technology major at Florida State University. Ever since I was young I've had a fascination with how computers work and intend on making a career out of it. Over the years I've earned my CompTIA A+ certification, worked on group development projects, gained experience with front and backend development, and am now working on expanding my github portfolio.

While a student at Florida State University I was hired as a IT Support Technican for the College of Communication and Information. While a technican at the CCI HelpDesk I was challenged to use my critical thinking skills to solve the problems the faculty/staff have with their technology. I also gained experience in providing remote experience when helping students troubleshoot their issues. When not working on tickets I'm responsible for maintaing the inventory management system for the college.
